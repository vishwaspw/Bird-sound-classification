About BirdCLEF 2025

BirdCLEF 2025 is an annual competition focused on developing AI models that can identify bird species by sound. This web application showcases our state-of-the-art bird sound classification system that can identify over 200 bird species.

Our system uses deep learning models trained on thousands of hours of bird recordings to provide accurate and reliable species identification. Whether you're a bird enthusiast, researcher, or nature lover, this tool can help you discover the birds around you.
